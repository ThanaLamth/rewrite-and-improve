# Coinlineup Rewrite Notes

## Site Positioning

`Coinlineup - Beginner crypto explainer`

Rewrite old news URLs into plain-English evergreen explainers that stay close to the original entity, topic, and backlink context.

## 1. U.S. Tariffs / IEEPA

- Old URL: `https://coinlineup.com/u-s-tariffs-shift-as-supreme-court-curbs-ieepa/`
- Direction: rewrite the existing URL directly rather than 410
- Why: the page has backlink value and should stay closely aligned with `tariffs`, `IEEPA`, and court-limit intent

### Recommended SEO Title

`Can a President Use Emergency Powers to Impose Tariffs? A Beginner's Guide to IEEPA`

### Recommended Meta Description

`Learn what IEEPA is, how U.S. emergency powers relate to tariffs, and why court challenges matter in plain English.`

### Recommended Angle

- Explain the issue for beginners, not legal specialists
- Keep the core entities from the original news piece
- Turn breaking-news framing into a durable explainer

### Suggested H2s

1. What Is IEEPA in Simple Terms?
2. What Are Tariffs?
3. Can Emergency Powers Be Used to Add Tariffs?
4. Why Do Court Challenges Matter?
5. How This Can Affect Markets and Crypto
6. FAQ

## Editorial Rule

For Coinlineup beginner explainers, the safest rewrite pattern is:

- keep the original entity
- lower the complexity
- convert news into a plain-English explainer
- avoid going so broad that the old backlink/topic match is lost
