# TheCCPress Content Roadmap 30-60-90

Date: 2026-06-11
Goal: shift visibility away from almost purely branded/homepage traffic and toward durable non-brand search demand

## Strategic Objective

Over the next 90 days, TheCCPress should stop spreading SEO effort across too many archives and instead:

1. stabilize a small hub set
2. publish a small number of evergreen pages with clear search intent
3. support those pages through internal links from high-crawl pages and relevant articles

## Hub Set To Support

Primary hubs:

1. `https://theccpress.com/latest-news/`
2. `https://theccpress.com/altcoin-news/`
3. `https://theccpress.com/crypto-101/`
4. `https://theccpress.com/learn-crypto/blockchain-technology/`

Secondary hubs:

1. `https://theccpress.com/top-picks/crypto-exchanges/`
2. `https://theccpress.com/press-releases/`
3. `https://theccpress.com/latest-news/bitcoin-news/`

## Days 1-30

### Objective

Create the first clean evergreen layer and make sure the supported hubs carry stable indexable signals.

### Deliverables

1. Confirm all priority hubs are live `200`, self-canonical, and indexable
2. Publish or refresh the first `5` evergreen pages
3. Add internal links from homepage modules and hub pages into those evergreen pages

### Priority Evergreen Pages

1. `what-is-cryptocurrency/`
2. `what-is-blockchain/`
3. `what-is-a-crypto-wallet/`
4. `what-is-a-crypto-exchange/`
5. `how-to-buy-crypto-safely/`

### Internal Linking Actions

1. Add persistent links from `crypto-101/` to all five pages
2. Add contextual links from `learn-crypto/blockchain-technology/` to `what-is-blockchain/`
3. Add links from `top-picks/crypto-exchanges/` to `what-is-a-crypto-exchange/` and `how-to-buy-crypto-safely/`
4. Add one homepage editorial block or sidebar block for beginner crypto guides

### Success Criteria

1. all five pages indexed cleanly
2. all five pages linked from at least three internal source pages each
3. no new duplicate URL variants introduced

## Days 31-60

### Objective

Extend from beginner foundations into practical commercial-intent and regulation explainers.

### Deliverables

Publish or refresh the next `5` evergreen pages:

6. `best-crypto-exchanges/`
7. `best-crypto-wallets/`
8. `custodial-vs-self-custody-wallets/`
9. `what-is-a-crypto-press-release/`
10. `how-crypto-regulation-affects-markets/`

### Internal Linking Actions

1. Route exchange-related articles into `best-crypto-exchanges/`
2. Route wallet-related articles into `best-crypto-wallets/` and `custodial-vs-self-custody-wallets/`
3. Route regulation and market-structure news into `how-crypto-regulation-affects-markets/`
4. Route press release and sponsored-content context into `what-is-a-crypto-press-release/`

### Hub Support Actions

1. improve copy depth on `top-picks/crypto-exchanges/`
2. improve copy depth on `crypto-101/`
3. add curated featured-guide modules on `latest-news/` and `altcoin-news/`

### Success Criteria

1. at least `10` evergreen pages total are live
2. each evergreen page has a clear primary parent hub
3. GSC begins to show impressions on non-home pages outside purely branded demand

## Days 61-90

### Objective

Consolidate the cluster, remove weak support patterns, and start measuring whether non-brand signals are diversifying.

### Actions

1. review GSC page and query exports again
2. identify which evergreen pages actually earn impressions
3. merge or deprioritize weak hubs that are still producing zero-value visibility
4. add second-wave internal links from newer articles into the pages that show traction
5. refine title tags and intros for evergreen pages that are visible but not winning clicks

### Optional Expansion

Only if the first ten pages stabilize well:

1. `best-altcoins-for-beginners/`
2. `how-crypto-wallet-security-works/`
3. `what-is-tokenized-stock-trading/`
4. `how-bitcoin-etfs-affect-price/`

### Success Criteria

1. homepage click concentration begins to weaken
2. category legacy variants stop appearing in performance exports
3. at least some evergreen pages begin to collect non-brand impressions and clicks

## What Not To Do During This Period

1. do not launch many new top-level categories
2. do not mass-produce thin news explainers with overlapping intent
3. do not spread links equally across too many archives
4. do not assume impressions on archive pages are the same as durable search growth

## Operating Rule

For the next 90 days, every new evergreen page should answer:

1. which hub owns this page?
2. which existing pages will link to it?
3. what non-brand search intent does it target?

If those answers are unclear, the page should not be a priority build.
